Government Consumer Complaint Helplines

National Consumer Helpline (NCH)

1915
Toll-free · 24×7 · 17 languages
Also: 1800-11-4000 · 14404
Portal: consumerhelpline.gov.in
Email: nch@nic.in
All consumer complaints — e-commerce, banking, telecom, insurance

RBI Integrated Ombudsman

14448
Toll-free · Mon–Fri 9 AM–5 PM
Portal: cms.rbi.org.in (24×7 online)
Covers: UPI failures, ATM issues, credit cards, home loans, bank charges
All scheduled banks, NBFCs, payment aggregators

IRDAI Insurance Grievance (Bima Bharosa)

155255
Alt: 1800-4254-732 (toll-free)
Portal: igms.irda.gov.in
Email: complaints@irdai.gov.in
Covers: all insurance — life, health, motor, travel

DGCA AirSewa (Aviation)

1800-11-1813
Toll-free
Portal: airsewa.gov.in
App: AirSewa (Android/iOS)
Covers: flight cancellation, delays, baggage, denied boarding

TRAI (Telecom Complaints)

1800-11-0019
Toll-free
DND Portal: dnd.trai.gov.in
Covers: spam calls/SMS, DND violations, service quality
For billing issues, use DoT CPGRAMS: pgportal.gov.in

IRCTC / Indian Railways

139
24×7 Railway Helpline
Also: 138 (parcel/goods), 182 (security)
Portal: railmaadad.indianrailways.gov.in
Covers: refunds, coach cleanliness, catering, ticketing

What to Do If Helplines Don't Work

If calling or emailing the company's grievance officer does not resolve your complaint within 15–30 days:

1. Send a legal notice — triggers formal legal responsibility
2. File with the NCH at consumerhelpline.gov.in / call 1915
3. Use the sector regulator (RBI, IRDAI, TRAI) for specialized complaints
4. File on EDAAKHIL (edaakhil.nic.in) for a binding Consumer Forum order

FAQs — Consumer Complaint Numbers India

What is the National Consumer Helpline number?
1915 — toll-free, 24×7, available in 17 languages including Hindi, English, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Gujarati, Kannada, and more.

What is the consumer complaint email ID?
National Consumer Helpline: nch@nic.in. Sector-specific: RBI → cms.rbi.org.in; IRDAI → igms.irda.gov.in or complaints@irdai.gov.in; TRAI → dnd.trai.gov.in; DGCA → airsewa.gov.in.

Is the National Consumer Helpline free to call?
Yes. 1915 is a toll-free number — no charge from any mobile or landline in India, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

What is the RBI consumer complaint number?
14448 (9 AM–5 PM, weekdays). For online filing 24×7: cms.rbi.org.in. Covers all bank-related disputes.
