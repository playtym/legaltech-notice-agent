How to File a SpiceJet Complaint & Send a Legal Notice

Are you facing flight cancellation and refund issues with SpiceJet? You are not alone. Thousands of consumers struggle with poor customer service every day.

If reaching out to SpiceJet customer care hasn't solved your problem, it's time to take legal action. Under the Consumer Protection Act, you have the right to claim a refund, replacement, and compensation for mental harassment.

Steps to escalate your complaint:

  1. Contact the SpiceJet Grievance Officer via email or registered post.
  2. Register your grievance on the INGRAM (National Consumer Helpline) portal.
  3. Send a formal pre-litigation legal notice to SpiceJet's registered office.

Sending a legally sound notice is often the fastest way to force companies like SpiceJet to take your complaint seriously and resolve the matter before it reaches the consumer court.
