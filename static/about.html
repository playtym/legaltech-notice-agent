Our Mission

In India, millions of consumers face unfair trade practices, missing refunds, and defective products every day. Traditional legal avenues are often too expensive, slow, or intimidating. At Lawly, our mission is to democratize legal access.

We built the first AI-powered legal notice generator specifically tailored to the Consumer Protection Act 2019. By automating the drafting process across 25+ specific Indian statutes, we make sending a legal notice fast, accurate, and affordable.

Why We Do It

A formal legal notice is often the push a company needs to resolve a dispute. Statistics show that up to 60% of consumer grievances are resolved out-of-court simply upon receiving a professionally formatted notice. Lawly bridges the gap between frustration and resolution.
