How to File a Consumer Complaint Against Ola Cabs

Unresolved Issue with Ola Cabs?

If Ola Cabs's customer support has failed to resolve your refund, wrong delivery, or service deficiency issue, your statutory right is to serve them with a formal legal notice.

Draft a Legal Notice to Ola Cabs in 2 Minutes →

1. Ola Cabs Customer Care & Helpline Details

Before taking legal action, ensure you have exhausted the standard support channels for Ola Cabs (Ride Hailing).

  • Contact their support via their official app or website help center.
  • Message them on official social media handles (Twitter/X is often monitored closely).
  • Always keep your Ticket IDs and screenshots of chats as evidence.

2. Ola Cabs Escalation Matrix & Grievance Officer

By Indian law (Consumer Protection E-Commerce Rules, 2020), digital platforms like Ola Cabs are required to appoint a Nodal Grievance Officer.

If standard customer care closes your ticket without a valid resolution, you must escalate the issue to the Grievance Officer. Provide your original Ticket ID and state the deficiency in service clearly.

3. Sending a Legal Notice to Ola Cabs

If the Grievance Officer fails to act within the prescribed time (usually 15-30 days), this constitutes grounds for legal acceleration. The next step is to send a formal legal notice to Ola Cabs's registered corporate office.

Why a Legal Notice Works:

  • Statutory Requirement: It formally notifies Ola Cabs of the impending Consumer Court case if the issue isn't settled.
  • Legal Department Priority: A drafted legal notice bypasses regular customer service bots and goes straight to their legal/compliance team.
  • Faster Settlement: Many companies prefer to settle valid grievances (e.g., return the money) once they receive a legally-sound notice rather than fighting in the National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission (NCDRC).

4. Using the National Consumer Helpline (NCH)

You can also register a parallel grievance via the Government of India's National Consumer Helpline (NCH) portal against Ola Cabs. In many cases, adding the NCH reference number into your legal notice strengthens your case significantly.
